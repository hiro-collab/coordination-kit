## Definition Of Playable

- launches locally in the browser
- player can understand the goal quickly
- player can complete or fail a short run
- controls are explained or discoverable
- the build exists by the midpoint check, even if rough
- the midpoint check can be demonstrated by a live end-to-end run

## Required Team Deliverables

- playable build
- short note describing chosen loop and scope cuts
- short note predicting what ordinary users are likely to judge most strongly
- short note describing where the player first sees rules and controls

## Suggested Scope Guardrails

- prefer placeholder visuals over large asset hunts
- prefer one clear loop over multiple unfinished systems
- cut menus, meta-progression, and extra modes before cutting the core playable loop
