- Readers:

- <phase> -> <node and owned outcome>

- <which files are now authoritative>

- <node> -> <what to revisit now>

- <node> -> <verification|documentation|localization|handoff evidence|other>

- <what is explicitly frozen or cut>

Deadline:
- <time or checkpoint>

Reply Required:
- <yes/no and what acknowledgment is needed>
